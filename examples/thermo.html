Example of thermo sudoku

(Featured in Cracking the Cryptic video A Great 'Thermo' Sudoku from one of our 'hottest' setters, by Phistomefel.)

Rules: normal sudoku rules apply, digits must increase along thermometers (starting at the bulb).

Use number keys to enter digits, Backspace to erase, hold Ctrl for center marks and Shift for corner marks.
